What is Budgeting?

Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend your money. It helps you:

  • Track income and expenses
  • Make informed decisions
  • Achieve financial goals
  • Avoid overspending

Core Components

Income

  • Salary
  • Wages
  • Investments

Expenses

  • Housing
  • Food
  • Transport

50/30/20 Rule
